Who we are

Turbulent markets require a consistent, structured wealth management approach.

Building significant wealth is the process of managing risk, not ignoring it. At NFB Private Wealth Managementstrong>, we guide clients through preparing their financial strategy and then help them stick to it with unwavering dedication.

Tough economic climates call for preparation and resilience to achieve results.

When markets are volatile and confidence is low, searching for reassurance is human instinct. Your path to future certainty lies in a financial plan crafted by a team of professional Wealth Managers with the skills and knowledge to empower you with the security you seek and the financial rewards you’ve earned.

The NFB approach

Unwavering dedication

  • Financial expertise starts with a relationship: we invest time in our clients, quality time getting to know their specific financial objectives; so that we can guide their journey with care and insight.
  • A truly tailored client experience:each financial journey is unique, and requires a bespoke strategy aligned to each client’s distinctive lifestyle and wealth ambitions.
  • Nothing but the best:we are proudly independent which allows us to be unbiased in the crafting of a product-based wealth management strategy for clients, matching the top offerings on the open market to their needs.
  • Cutting the complexity out of wealth management: our highly-qualified Wealth Managers advise and enlighten clients, giving them the ability and peace of mind to make informed decisions.
  • Taking care of wealth management strategy execution:Thanks to Cat. 2 FAIS licenses, we’re able to make discretionary calls for our clients; giving them peace of mind that they can focus on other priorities, while we handle the necessary investment allocation decisions in their portfolios.

What can our clients
expect from us?

Our seasoned approach to wealth management, developed face to face with clients, ensures a truly personalised blueprint for achieving future growth and financial security.

  • We provide sound financial advice tailored on an appreciation for their needs with due consideration given to economic and market factors
  • We deliver advice with empathy and bring rational thought, care and knowledge to counterbalance investor emotion
  • We ensure a personal touch while giving clear, concise guidance to align portfolio performance with client expectations
  • We help clients to prepare for every eventuality, so that they can stick to their wealth management plan
  • We offer a product-based strategy to fit every requirement, to ensure we can provide clients with the best options for every investment objective
